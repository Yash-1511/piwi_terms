Privacy Policy for Piwi Chrome Extension

Last Updated: February 22, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Piwi Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following types of information:

  • LinkedIn Profile Information:
    • First and last name
    • Profile headline
    • Profile image
    • LinkedIn member ID
  • Authentication Information:
    • LinkedIn cookies for maintaining connection between LinkedIn and Piwi portal

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Connecting your LinkedIn account with the Piwi portal
  • Enabling sales professionals to effectively manage their LinkedIn connections
  • Maintaining the functionality of the Extension

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell or transfer your data to third parties
  • Use or transfer your data for purposes unrelated to the Extension's core functionality
  • Use or transfer your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
